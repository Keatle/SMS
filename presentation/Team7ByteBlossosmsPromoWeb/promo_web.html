Our Vision

Our vision is to provide a vibrant and inclusive online platform that fosters community, streamlines society administrative tasks, and reduces manual work.

We aim to create a system that promotes collaboration, transparency, and efficiency.

This will make society participation enjoyable and stress-free for both students and administrators.

Current System

The manual processing of society registrations and administrative tasks burdens Mrs. Thekiso, leading to delays and inefficiencies.

Students experience delays in inquiries and have to physically visit the office for assistance.

Our Solution

An integrated online platform for society management, streamlining registration processes, simplifying administration, and providing centralized communication for all society leaders and their members.

System Features

Member Management

Allows students to join societies, manage membership requests, and view society details.

Event Management

Enables societies to create and manage events, send invitations, track RSVPs, and take attendance by generating an attendance code for students present at the event.

Task Assignment

Facilitates assigning tasks to society members and tracking their progress through a central dashboard.

Financial Management

Handles society budgets, financial requests, fund status, and allocations.

Stakeholder Roles

Member

Able to view upcoming events and RSVP to one of their choice

Marks presence for attendance at a particular society event using our system

Chairperson

Manages society members, assigns leadership roles, and monitors activity of the society's leadership committee.

Secretary

Responsible for sending event invites, taking attendance, and updating the notice board.

Treasurer

Handles society funds, budget plans, and submission of financial requests.

Admin

Responsible for managing all existing societies and their leaders, society registration requests, society leadership role assignments, financial and event requests, and analyzing society activities.
